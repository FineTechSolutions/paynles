🧠 The Magic

Here’s what Paynles will do (in plain English):

  • Browse without logging in. Because friction kills good ideas.
  • Sign up when you’re ready. But we promise not to ask for your blood type.
  • Add items from anywhere. Paste a link. Scan a receipt. Yell it at your phone (coming soon).
  • We’ll track the price. On multiple sites. In real-time. With smarter alerts.
  • When it drops — boom — you buy.
  • Or don't. Maybe you just wanted to feel powerful.

And here’s the kicker…

  • You can switch modes like Batman.
    • Personal Shopper by day.
    • Procurement Officer by night.
    • Family Bargain Ninja on weekends.

Each mode has its own brain.
Switch between them like Google accounts — no identity crisis necessary.

💡 Big Brain Moves
