Paynles.com Internal Instruction Sheet – Master Checklist

MISSION
Paynles.com helps people and businesses save money automatically by tracking product prices, suggesting cheaper alternatives, and managing shopping intelligently — all through a fun, effortless user experience.

CORE PLATFORM STRUCTURE

Anonymous Use (No Sign-Up)
- Users can browse trending deals and sample savings.
- Limited tracking is allowed without an account.
- Encouragement to sign up is light and benefit-driven.

Simple Sign-Up (Progressive Onboarding)
- Google / Apple / Email login only.
- No mandatory forms — profile builds over time.
- Onboarding: interests, first item, choose mode.

ACCOUNT MODES (USER SPACES)
Users can manage multiple independent accounts:
1. Personal
2. Couple
3. Family
4. Business (Solo)
5. Business (Team)

Each has separate: wish list, alerts, preferences, history.

Cross-Mode & Sharing Features
- Move/copy items between accounts.
- Role-based permissions (Viewer / Editor / Admin).
- Collaborate and comment.
- Public curated lists in future.

INTELLIGENT TRACKING
- Paste a link, scan receipt, or import history.
- AI-powered alerts for price drops, alternatives, bundles.
- Target-price notifications.
- Historical trend tracking.

BROWSER EXTENSION & MOBILE SHARING
Chrome Extension:
- Scrape any product page.
- Save to any list/account.
Mobile Share Integration:
- Show Paynles in share sheet.
- Extract product info and allow tracking.

SUBSCRIPTION TIERS

Free: Basic tracking, 1 mode.
Pro: Unlimited items, history, smart suggestions.
Premium: Multi-mode, shared lists, early deal access.

SPECIAL FEATURES (FUTURE)
- Smart lists: Pantry, Closet, Garage
- Reorder prompts
- Expense tagging
- Bulk optimization
- Shopping timeline suggestions

MINI-SITE FOR AFFILIATE VERIFICATION
Required before applying to: Amazon, Walmart, Home Depot, Staples

Must Include:
- Home
- About Us
- How It Works
- Sample Deals
- Affiliate Disclosure
- Contact Page
- Privacy / Terms
- (Optional) Blog, Testimonials, FAQ

DEV IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

TEAM REMINDERS
- Keep it fast, intuitive, and fun.
- Clean visuals with personality.
- Savings-first messaging.
- Less friction = more users.
